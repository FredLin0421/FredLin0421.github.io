GNN Resilience

Dec 2022 — Fault tolerance in graphs

Project Summary

GNN Resilience explores fault tolerance in graph neural networks. The project analyzes the effects of faults on graph-based models and develops methods to enhance their robustness in real-world applications.

My Role

Researcher: designed fault injection experiments, evaluated GNN performance, and contributed to resilience strategies.

Technologies Used

  • Python, PyTorch Geometric
  • Graph Neural Networks
  • Fault Injection, Robustness Analysis

Details

The project involved introducing faults into graph data and GNN architectures, then measuring the impact on model accuracy and stability. The research led to new techniques for improving the reliability of GNNs in safety-critical domains.
